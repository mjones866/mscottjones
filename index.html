About

A Curious problem solver by nature. A designer & developer by trade.

My name is Matt and I am a UI/UX designer, developer, photographer and maker of things living on the east side of Atlanta. On the weekends, you can usually find me staying fit or working on some sort of DIY project. I have a deep love and passion for furniture building and design.

With over 5 years of experience in the industry, I continuously learn everyday especially in the development field. As a designer, I error on the side of minimalism and apply the "less is more" mentality when and where I can. My belief is that this credo spans out beyond design.
